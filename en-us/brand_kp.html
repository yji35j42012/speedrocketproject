Brand Profile

Fragrance is part of the first impression you leave, often crucial to any interaction. We realized that renowned perfumes are often very expensive, and therefore hard for first time users to start. We therefore decided to create a perfume with outstanding quality but an entry level price tag. Klower Pandor was born with the concept of Pandora’s Flower Knowledge, and we hope like the box, each will open a whole new scenario for you to explore. What we are creating at KP is a fragrance for you to keep, and create a memory for you and the people you encounter to cherish.

Products

A perfume you’ll easily fall in love with. With a 15% fragrance concentrate, the scent will linger a good 4 to 6 hours, and thus is perfect for first timers that are not used to carrying a scent. 12 different fragrance profiles are suitable for every scenario from dates to parties, to friends’ gatherings to the workplace. Allowing everyone to find their first-time perfume profile.
Perfumers from France and England had been hired to create these diffuser blends. The diffusers were also the only Taiwanese fragrances selected by the Golden Horse Movie Awards in their goodie bags three years in a row. It’s the 5th most searched for brand on the local market, and even fashion directors have fallen under its spell. With accumulated sales of 500,000 bottles, the ingredients have been US NSF accredited, and are suitable for most people, even if pregnant. A natural enhancer of your daily life, soothing the soul in its low-key way.
Treating the skin on the body as if it were the delicate facial area, with an upgraded formula and fragrance profile, allowing shower time to become a calming, peaceful spa time. The nourishing ingredients cleanse the skin while also moisturizing it, leaving the body clean and supple. The scent lingers mildly on the body, allowing bath time to become your personal care time.
