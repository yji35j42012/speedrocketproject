Company Profile

Speeding Rocket was founded in 2016, by a Taiwanese designer and a marketing team, where we established our own fragrance brand, and utilized our e-commerce business operation experience, to develop Super Landing Shop Platform as well as the X-Delivery Logistics Information Platform.
The team continues to focus on market trends and has collaborated with a number of brands from different countries. We have launched product lines in fragrance cards, perfume, toiletries such as shower gel, lotions, as well as essential oils. We have also successfully branched out with our clothing line, where we invited E.SO as brand ambassadors.
6 years after our founding, Speeding Rocket has established 15 storefronts at various department stores and malls. In 2022, we branched out to overseas markets such as Malaysia, Hong Kong, Singapore and Japan, where we were able to replicate our success in Taiwan.

Businesses Include:

Brand Operations
  • Speeding Rocket as established two fragrance brands Shareco and Klower Pandor, with product lines such as male and female perfumes, diffusers, toiletries, essential oils and clothing and accessories, with a current presence in 15 department stores and malls. We have invited renowned celebrities E.SO as brand ambassadors, and in 2022, set up overseas store fronts in Malaysia, Singapore, Hong Kong and Japan, gradually working our way towards international marketing.
  • In 2021, we acquired the food brand Lao Peng Yu, known for their special secret duck web appetizers, currently we have storefronts in 6 department stores.
Software Development
  • With our own operating know-how, Speeding Rocket has released our self-developed software system as a SaaS platform for small and medium-sized e-commerce, including Super Landing Shop Platform’ and ‘X-Delivery Logistics Information Platform’ so that other e-commerce businesses can also use our experience in e-commerce tools.
Branding & Marketing
  • Having witnessed the e-commerce boom period, crossed over a hundred competing brands, and then moved into the post-pandemic era, Speeding Rocket's marketing capabilities are well recognized by the industry, and we have been working with Jean Kao’s cosmetic brand J.GAO, Taiwanese original women’s apparel brand H&J, and influencer Yu Kai for a long time to provide overall brand marketing services and continue to pursue a win-win situation and achieve record results.
