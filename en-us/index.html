SPEEDING ROCKET

Fragrance Branding Marketing Software

Speeding Rockets offers fragrance products, branding and marketing, e-commerce platform and system software solutions. With the integration of domestic and overseas O2O online and offline channels, we can maximize the benefits of brand-named e-commerce. We are a company that skillfully integrates ‘emotional connections’ with ‘rational logic’.

Branding Power Software Technology Service Omni-Channel

Since starting out in 2016, we have established our own brands with SHARECO and KLOWER PANDOR, as well as acquiring the food brand LAO PENG YU. Our product development focuses on design and quality, and we continue to improve the information flow, efficiency, and interfacing between platforms. Smart and automated logistics and storage, and a customer service with high satisfaction, these four key aspects has led to our year on year 10% revenue growth. In 2019, we established YBG.inc as the brick-and-mortar branding umbrella, and officially entered the O2O omni-channel, neo-retail business model. Currently we have a presence in 19 different storefronts and are branching out to the overseas markets in Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong and Japan.

A hybrid of banding and information technology,
offering an intuitive ecommerce platform

Speeding Rocket uses our own business experience as the basis and aims to develop an e-commerce platform that ‘increases all efficiencies x reduces all losses’. With branding marketing and business operations in mind, we have developed several e-commerce software tools such as: SUPER LANDING One-Page Shop Platform and X-DELIVERY Logistics Information Platform. Utilizing our own brands’ actual business experience and know-how, we are consolidating software and tooling, creating intuitive, user-friendly platforms.

Assists brands in growing together

We excel in brand marketing, and influencer assisted marketing strategies. Coupled with our software, logistics and cashflow capabilities, as well as operational know-how, we can utilize practical experience in assisting brands in rapid growth of revenue. We have long-term partnership with celebrity Jean Gao’s cosmetics brand J.GAO, Taiwanese original women’s apparel brand H&J, and renowned influencer Yu Kai’s brand YOUR KIND. Our collaboration includes branding and marketing, generating the maximum impact across platforms.
