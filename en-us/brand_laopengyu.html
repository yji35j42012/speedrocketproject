Brand Profile

It all started because we couldn’t forget that appetizer our uncle served us in Yilan. We now source the best and freshest ingredients, every piece of garlic, every drop of sauce, is our mission statement to hold true to the original. Led by a veteran chef, their signature dish is now conquering the e-commerce market, bringing the Yilan local delicacies to everyone’s table through the internet. Currently we also have four department storefronts, and in 2022, our very own flagship store opened in Kaohsiung.

Products

With the lead chef’s 30 years of experience in the business, this series has conquered the tastebuds of thousands of foodies. Popular Products

  • Top 1. Secret Spicy Duck Feet

    Our most popular product. Spicy and crunchy, with chocolate habanero and garlic from Yunlin, the heat will slowly build up in your mouth! Full of collagen and protein, the duck feet are a popular treat along with the crispy cucumber pieces, definitely a wow on the table.

  • Top 2. Sour & Spicy Seaweed

    Brand new product for 2022, only 100 jars are made every day. A refreshing ginger and vinegar flavor, with crunchy seaweed and a light tang, appetizing and making the rice disappear all the faster!

  • Top 3. Sour Garlic Duck

    Smoked with sugarcane in the traditional way for 3.5 hours, the duck breast slices are super juicy and sweet, with fresh garlic leek that packs a punch, and rice vinegar that assists the appetite, it’s a favorite amongst those that don’t like spicy food.
