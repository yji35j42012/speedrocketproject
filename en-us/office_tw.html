YBG.inc

In 2019, we created the retail umbrella brand YBG to lead Shareco and Klower Pandor into department stores all over Taiwan. At these storefronts, we provide consumers with a full experience of our products and brand culture, and use the first-line feedback and suggestions received as nutrients for further product optimization. YBG currently has 15 storefronts in department stores throughout Taiwan and has opened its first flagship store in Ximending.
