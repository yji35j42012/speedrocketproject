System Profile

As our brands grew, and the number of users of Super Landing Shop Platform, we realized how logistics impact the running of an e-commerce business. In 2018, in partnership with Super Landing Re-Yi Distributions, we established X-Delivery Logistics, a logistics information platform based on our own operational experience. It consolidates receipt, dispatch, label printing, and SMS tracking functions, and interfaces seamlessly with Super Landing, Shopee, and a number of other Taiwanese online shopping platforms, allowing the effective gathering of storage and logistics data such as package dispatch numbers, package refusal numbers, and stock storage and disposal numbers. Economical, user friendly and intuitive, it allows for better efficiency of the packaging team. Optimizing a hidden operation cost, and creating more room for profit.
