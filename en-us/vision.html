We want to become a company where our employees find recognition, passion and a sense of accomplishment

Speeding Rocket wants to establish the corporate culture of:
Not self-limitations, integrity, honesty, rapid response, flexibility, speak up, and offer solutions. All people aboard this Speeding Rocket will find a place where they can take center stage and reach new heights and depths they have not been able to reach before.

” Fragrance Culture Stories ”

Steve Jobs once said that “creativity is just connecting things”. We are combining the sensory abstract of perfumes and fragrances with emotional stories, as well as visual stories, psychology, culture and business strategies; thus, we are creating a lifestyle that is attractive and inviting. With the use of this fragrance, people can elevate themselves.

“ High Operational Efficiency Storefront Platform ”

We focus on software, on business procedures, and established our own e-commerce platform. When sales are maximized, good software and procedure, as well as transparent information will be able to further maximize the operational efficiency, and thus reach the maximized operational effectiveness.

In the End, the Brand that Conquered the World

Our goal is to push our products,
our software, and eventually,
our knowledge and experience to the world!
