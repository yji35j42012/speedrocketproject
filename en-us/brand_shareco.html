Brand Profile

The sense of smell is one of the most primal of human senses, triggering memories and desires of the brain. A perfume, therefore, is a way to send subtle messages, interpreted without a conscious thought. Our vision is that every fragrance we produce will illicit a charming memory for people you encounter. The scents are for those who know how to wield them. If you believe in intuitive actions, Shareco is the one for you.

Products

Recommended by popular Taiwanese rapper E.SO, reported by fashion magazines such as GQ, Men’s Uno, Cool… etc., Shareco hired a renowned perfumer for this particular endeavor, where we use imported fragrances for our unique blends at a 30% concentration, allowing for up to 8 hours of lingering scent. We have since accumulated over 200,000 bottles in sales!
With 90% of our customers coming back for more, these shower gels have a high percentage of fragrance content, coupled with plant extracts. The fragrance lingers naturally on the skin after the shower, leaving the skin feel clean, supple and smooth. The products have passed SGS examinations and are of a mild acidity suitable for all skin types. With added licorice root extract and other plant extracts, the products offer a mild nourishing of the skin during showering.
The Shareco clothing line has the concept of ‘tailored comfort’ at its heart. The Couture At Ease line does away with all the flourishes, and focuses on good quality material and production procedures to showcase its luxuriousness. Using soft, breathable fabric that can sustain a beating, coupled with the cold color tones, our clothing patterns, materials, and colors are the best they could be. The simplicity of their design makes them pretty much suitable for all kinds of occasions, even E.SO is a big fan!
